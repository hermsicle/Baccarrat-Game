How to play Bacarrat

  • Player will choose to bet on player or banker
  • Click 'Bet player' or 'Bet banker'
  • Game will distrubute cards to player side and banker side
  • Whichever side gets the highest number wins
  • The highest number is 9.
  • If the cards distrubuted total sum is less than 7, dealer will add another card
  • Max total of cards is 3
  • If cards added exeeds 10 then the remaining numbers will be added from 0
  • Ex: 8 + 8 = 6 || 7 + 8 = 5
  • If you betted on player and player has the higher cards, you get one point
  • If you betted on player and banker has higher cards, the banker gets one point
  • If you betted on banker and banker has the higher cards, you get one point
  • If you betted on banker and player has the higher cards, banker get one point
  • Game will reset after you reach 8 points
  • Good Luck!
